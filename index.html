About Me


Hello! My name is Jabez Nuetey(Jay). As a kid living in Ghana, West Africa, my interest in computer science grew stronger as I progressed through school. In high school, my favorite show was HBO's Silicon Valley. That show expanded my love for computer science and programming more than ever. After graduating High School, I had to make the hard decision of leaving my country to pursue computer science in the United States. This decision came with its fair share of adversaries. I overcame a lot of challenges and with perseverance, I am now in my senior year at Missouri State University.
